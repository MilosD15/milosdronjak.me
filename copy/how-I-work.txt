[approach]

item_1_body: Before introducing anything new, I look at the tools your clients are already paying for, the apps, the platforms, the subscriptions, and ask how far we can get with those. New monthly costs only come in when there's no other way.

item_3_body: Wonderful Dental. Zoe Rowyn Bridal. E-Flight Academy. MKB Digiwerkplaats. Match-IT. Not experiments, production, with real people on the other end waiting for it to work.
